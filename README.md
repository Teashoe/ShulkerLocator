Automatically records the coordinates of the shulker box you place. <br>
Records are immediately deleted when you break a box.

## How to Use
Type **/shulkerlocate**

**Only shulker boxes placed after installing the mod will be tracked**

![1](https://cdn.modrinth.com/data/cached_images/f041cab9a4c49db612f291ab11fc71bae0c50a28.png)


If another player moves your box while you're away, the mod will detect the missing block when you get close and automatically update your list.
